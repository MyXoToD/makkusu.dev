Japanese

In october 2020 I started learning Japanese. Besides learning Hiragana and Katakana, I used apps like memrise to get basic vocabulary. After feeling confident with most of the Kana, I started creating my own Anki decks and started using more apps. A list of all apps, websites and others tools I used to study you can find below.

Magic

I have never been not mesmerized by seeing magic or sleight of hand. When I was a kid I loved watching the masked magician to see how the secrets work and then practice and perform some of them to my mother and friends.

At one point I bought my very first custom playing card deck and I studied card tricks and sleight moves for a really long time. I never lost my interest in magic but it wasn't until my early 20s that I activly joined the magic circle and upped my skills even more.

Magic is still one of my most beloved hobby but currently I'm taking a break from practice and due to corona it is difficult to go out and perform. Maybe I will return performing more often in the future.
